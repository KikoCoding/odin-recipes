Strawberry Smoothie


Description

Get your fruit fix with our strawberry smoothie made with banana and orange juice. It's free from dairy, so it's vegan too â€“ making it a great start to anyone's day.


Ingredients


  • 10 strawberries, hulled (approx 175g)
  • 1 small banana, sliced
  • 100ml orange juice, chilled

Steps


  1. Blitz the strawberries in a blender with the banana and orange juice until smooth.
  2. Pour the smoothie into a tall glass to serve.
